Fret not! There are various places where pickups (informal games) are organised regularly, so do check out this page often to look out for the latest updates!

Competitions

Every year, there are many competitions in Singapore catering to the different parts of the Ultimate community. On the national level, there are various competitions such as Mixed Nationals, Gendermah and Singapore Ultimate Open (SUO). On the school level, the yearly Inter-Junior College Ultimate Championship and POL-ITE Ultimate Frisbee Tournament provides opportunities for students to compete with other schools as well!
